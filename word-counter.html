Understanding the Numbers

Words: We define a word as any sequence of characters separated by spaces. That means “can’t” and “hello123” each count as one word.

Characters with spaces: Every single character in the text, including spaces, line breaks, and punctuation.

Characters without spaces: Only letters, digits, and symbols — spaces and line breaks are removed.

Sentences: We split text at periods, exclamation marks, and question marks followed by a space or line break.

Paragraphs: Any block of text separated by one or more blank lines is counted as a paragraph.

Reading time: Calculated as word count / 200 (the average adult reading speed for English).

Word Counter FAQs – Questions? We’ve Got Answers

Does the word counter include spaces in the character count?
We show both character counts – with spaces and without spaces – so you can see exactly what you need for different platforms.
How is reading time calculated?
Reading time is based on an average speed of 200 words per minute, the widely accepted standard for general English text.
Can I count words in other languages?
Yes! The tool uses smart word boundary detection that works with most scripts, including Chinese, Arabic, and Hindi. For CJK characters, each character is counted as a separate word.
Is my text safe?
Absolutely. All processing happens in your browser. Your text never leaves your device, and we never store or see anything you paste.
