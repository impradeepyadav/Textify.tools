What Is a URL Slug?

A URL slug is the part of a web address that comes after the domain name and identifies a specific page or post in a human‑readable way. For example, in the URL https://example.com/blog/seo-tips-for-beginners, the slug is seo-tips-for-beginners. Good slugs are short, descriptive, and use hyphens to separate words – which is exactly what this tool helps you create.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the generator free?
Yes, completely free. No registration, no hidden costs.
Can I generate slugs for 100+ titles at once?
Absolutely! Switch to Bulk Mode, paste your titles line by line, and click Generate. You'll get all slugs instantly.
Does the tool work offline?
Once the page is loaded, all slug generation happens inside your browser. You can even disconnect from the internet and it will still work.
Can I use underscores instead of hyphens?
Absolutely. Choose “Underscore” from the separator dropdown. However, for public URLs, hyphens are recommended for better SEO.
What are stop words and why remove them?
Stop words are common words like “a”, “the”, “in”, “on”. Removing them makes slugs shorter and more keyword‑rich without changing the meaning.
Is my data private?
Yes, absolutely. All text processing is done client‑side – your text never leaves your device.
