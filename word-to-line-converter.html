Word to Line Converter

Split any text by words and instantly put each word on a new line. Perfect for creating vertical lists, preparing data, or reformatting content.

💡 Tip: This tool splits text by words (using spaces and punctuation as separators). Each word appears on a separate line. Perfect for cleaning up messy data, creating to-do lists, or preparing content for further processing.

How to Use the Word to Line Converter

1

Type or Paste Your Text

Enter any block of text. It can be a sentence, paragraph, or an entire document. The tool will split it by words regardless of length.

2

Choose Options (Optional)

Trim extra spaces, change case (uppercase/lowercase), skip empty lines, or add line numbers. All options are optional — you can also just click convert to see the basic vertical list.

3

Click “Convert to Lines”

The tool instantly processes your text and displays the result in the output box below. You'll also see word count and character statistics.

4

Copy or Download Your Result

Copy the vertical word list to your clipboard or download it as a .txt file for use in spreadsheets, emails, or any other application.

Common Use Cases

  • Preparing import lists: Many applications (e.g., mailing lists, CMS keywords) require one item per line. Splitting a sentence into words gives you the perfect structure.
  • Creating checklists: A sentence like "Write article, edit images, publish" becomes a vertical checklist you can copy into a note‑taking app.
  • Text analysis: Convert a large paragraph into a word‑by‑word list for frequency analysis or manual review.
  • Data cleaning: Separate words in a dataset for further processing (e.g., deduplication or normalization).
  • Fun and educational: Help learners break down sentences into individual words.

Advanced Options Explained

  • Trim spaces: Removes leading and trailing spaces from each line (keeps the line clean when split).
  • Convert to lowercase / uppercase: Normalizes case for further processing or uniform display.
  • Skip empty lines: Automatically removes any empty lines that might appear due to multiple spaces or line breaks in the input.
  • Add line numbers: Prepends each line with a sequential index (1, 2, 3 …) for easy reference.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?
Yes, it's completely free. No login, no premium plan, no hidden fees.
Does my data stay private?
Absolutely. Everything runs in your browser – your text never leaves your device. No server uploads, no logs.
What counts as a "word"?
We split by whitespace and punctuation (spaces, newlines, commas, periods, etc.). This means "hello,world" becomes two lines: "hello" and "world".
How large can the input be?
The tool is optimized for large texts; however, extremely long inputs (millions of words) might be slower on older devices. For normal usage (up to tens of thousands of words), it works instantly.
