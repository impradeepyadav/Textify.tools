📖 How to Use the Advanced Lorem Ipsum Generator

1
Choose a style
Pick from 6 different word banks: Classic Latin, Hipster, Tech, Corporate, Nature, or Kitchen Ipsum. Each gives your placeholder text a unique flavour that fits your project’s mood.
2
Set the structure
Adjust the number of paragraphs (1–30) and the min/max words per paragraph. This ensures realistic variation – no two paragraphs will be identical in length.
3
Add advanced components
Check “Include random headings (H2)” to insert relevant subheadings. Enable “Include occasional bullet lists” to mimic real content with unordered lists. You can also bold the first sentence of the first paragraph for emphasis.
4
Toggle output format
Use the “Show HTML” / “Show Plain Text” button to switch between raw text and ready‑to‑use HTML markup (with <p>, <h2>, and <ul> tags).
5
Generate & copy
Click “Generate Advanced” to create your text. Then copy it directly to your clipboard or download it as a .txt file – all processing stays in your browser, so your data is completely private.

💡 Pro tip: For web design mockups, enable HTML mode and copy the generated markup directly into your HTML editor. For document placeholders, keep plain text mode and adjust the word range to match your layout.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between styles?
Each style uses a different word bank. Classic is standard Latin placeholder text; Hipster uses modern slang; Tech focuses on digital/IT terms; Corporate uses business jargon; Nature uses outdoor vocabulary; Kitchen uses culinary terms.
Can I use the HTML output in my project?
Absolutely. Toggle to HTML mode and copy the generated markup – it includes headings, paragraphs and lists with clean structure.
Is everything private?
Yes, all generation happens inside your browser. No data leaves your device.
What does “Start with Lorem ipsum…” do?
When using the Classic style, this option ensures the very first paragraph begins with the traditional “Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet…” opening line, which many designers prefer for authenticity.
