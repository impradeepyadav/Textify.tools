Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

👋 A quick note from our team: We built Textify.tools because we were tired of text tools that demanded sign‑ups and quietly sent our data to some server. So we made a promise: your text never leaves your browser. We don't have a database to store it, we don't log it, and we certainly don't sell it. This privacy policy explains the few things we do collect (like anonymous page views) and why. If you have questions, drop us a message — we read every email.

1. The Short Version

No accounts. No personal data collected. Any text you type stays on your device. We use Google Analytics to see how many people visit (anonymously) and Google AdSense (plus a few other ad partners) to show ads. That's how we keep the lights on.

2. What We Never Collect

  • No personal information: We don't ask for your name, email, or password.
  • No user content: Everything you type into our tools is processed locally in your browser. It never reaches our servers. You can even use the tools offline.

3. What We Do Collect (Automatically)

Like most websites, our server automatically receives some basic technical information when you visit. This includes:

  • IP address (anonymized)
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Referring page
  • Date and time of visit
  • Pages viewed and time spent

We use this data to understand site traffic and fix bugs. It's never linked to any individual person.

4. Cookies

We use a few cookies to make the site work smoothly:

  • Essential cookie: Remembers your dark/light mode preference.
  • Analytics cookies (Google Analytics): Help us count visitors.
  • Advertising cookies (Google AdSense, etc.): Let our ad partners show relevant ads.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings anytime. The tools will still work perfectly — you'll just see the default light theme each time.

5. Third‑Party Services We Use

To keep Textify.tools free, we work with a few trusted partners. Here's exactly who they are and what they do:

  • Google Analytics: Tracks anonymous visitor statistics (like which pages are popular). Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google AdSense: Displays ads. Google may use cookies to show ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out of personalized ads at Google Ad Settings.
  • Ezoic (if active): An ad management platform that also uses cookies for ad delivery and analytics. Ezoic Privacy Policy.
  • Gatekeeper Consent: Shows the cookie consent popup when you first visit. It respects your choice about which cookies you allow. Gatekeeper Privacy Policy.

These third parties have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them. We don't have access to the data they collect, and we don't sell any data to anyone (we have none to sell).

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Because we don't store personal data, there's nothing to delete or export. But we fully respect your rights under GDPR (Europe) and CCPA (California). If you have any privacy concern, just contact us — we'll respond honestly, usually within a day.

7. Children's Privacy

Our tools are for everyone, but we never knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have, let us know and we'll take immediate action.

8. External Links

We sometimes link to other websites. We can't control their privacy practices, so please check their policies when you visit.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we ever change how we handle data, we'll update this page. Big changes might get a notice on the homepage, but we're a small team — we don't like surprises either.

10. Contact & Consent

By using Textify.tools, you agree to this policy. If you don't agree, that's okay — but we hope you'll stick around.

Thanks for trusting us with your text. We take that responsibility seriously.
— The Textify.tools Team
