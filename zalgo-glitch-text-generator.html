What is Zalgo / Glitch Text?

Zalgo text (also called "glitch text", "corrupted text", or "creepy text") is a stylistic effect created by adding Unicode combining diacritical marks to standard letters. These marks attach above or below the base character, making the text appear chaotic, distorted, or even "cursed". The name originates from an internet meme about a demonic entity, and the effect is wildly popular in gaming, horror stories, and social media pranks.

Our generator uses over 150 unique combining characters from Unicode ranges U+0300–U+036F and U+20D0–U+20FF. You control the intensity – from a light, eerie flicker to an unreadable storm of marks. All transformations run entirely in your browser; your text never leaves your device.
