Your Privacy Matters

Many online tools require you to upload files or process data on their servers. Textify is different. Every tool is built with client-side JavaScript – meaning your data is processed entirely inside your browser and never sent anywhere.

  • No server logs – we don't even see your text.
  • No account needed – just open the page and use it.
  • No tracking cookies – only anonymous analytics for page views.
  • Works offline? Once loaded, many tools continue to work even if you lose internet.
