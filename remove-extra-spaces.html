Remove Extra Spaces – Clean Up Messy Text Instantly

Paste your text and let the tool remove extra spaces, trim leading/trailing whitespace, collapse multiple spaces into one, delete blank lines, and more. All processing stays in your browser — private and instant.

How to Use the Remove Extra Spaces Tool

Paste your messy text, choose a cleaning mode, and click "Clean Text". The result appears instantly below. You can then copy or download the cleaned version. The original text remains unchanged in the input box, so you can try multiple modes without losing your data.

What Each Cleaning Mode Does

  • Multiple spaces to single: Replaces any sequence of spaces, tabs, or line breaks with a single space, while also trimming leading and trailing whitespace. Ideal for normalizing paragraphs.
  • Trim leading & trailing only: Removes spaces only at the very beginning and very end of your text. Useful for cleaning up copied cells from spreadsheets.
  • Remove all spaces: Strips every space character, leaving the text completely compact. Great for generating slugs or compact codes.
  • Remove line breaks (join lines): Turns multi‑line text into one continuous block, separated by spaces.
  • Convert tabs to spaces: Replaces tab characters with a single space, while preserving existing spaces.
  • Full clean (all of the above): Collapses all whitespace into single spaces, trims, and converts tabs — a complete reset for your text.

Why Clean Up Extra Spaces?

Extra spaces can sneak into your text when copying from PDFs, emails, or chat logs. They make documents look sloppy and can cause formatting issues. This tool helps you quickly restore clean, professional‑looking text without manually hunting for every double space. Since it runs entirely in your browser, your data never leaves your device.

Real‑World Use Cases

  • Copy‑pasting from PDFs: PDFs often insert random spaces or line breaks. Our tool cleans them up in one click.
  • Preparing data for spreadsheets: Remove hidden spaces that break sorting or filtering.
  • Cleaning code snippets: Normalize indentation and remove accidental blank lines.
  • Formatting chat logs: Join fragmented lines into readable paragraphs.
  • Before running other tools: Use this as a pre‑processing step before a word counter, case converter, or text sorter for more accurate results.
