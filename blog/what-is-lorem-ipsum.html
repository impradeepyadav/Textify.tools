What Is Lorem Ipsum and When to Use It

📅 April 12, 2026

You've seen it a hundred times: the scrambled Latin‑looking text that fills a mockup before the real content arrives. It's called Lorem Ipsum, and it's been the design world's favorite placeholder for over 500 years. But what exactly is it, where does it come from, and when should you use it?

In this guide we'll uncover the story behind Lorem Ipsum, explain its purpose in modern web and graphic design, and show you how to generate perfect placeholder text instantly with the Textify.tools Lorem Ipsum Generator.

The Origin of Lorem Ipsum

Contrary to popular belief, Lorem Ipsum is not random gibberish. It is derived from sections 1.10.32–1.10.33 of De Finibus Bonorum et Malorum ("On the Ends of Good and Evil"), a philosophical work by the Roman statesman Cicero written in 45 BC.

The original passage begins:

"Neque porro quisquam est, qui dolorem ipsum quia dolor sit amet, consectetur, adipisci velit…"

Roughly translated: "There is no one who loves pain itself, who seeks after it and wants to have it, simply because it is pain…"

The passage was discovered in a book of type specimens in the 1500s, and later popularized by Letraset transfer sheets in the mid‑20th century and by desktop publishing software like Aldus PageMaker in the 1980s.

💡 Fun fact: The original text has been altered from the classical Latin – words like "consectetur" were modified for typographic purposes, so while it looks like Latin, it's not exactly classical Latin anymore.

Why Do Designers Use Lorem Ipsum?

The primary reason is that readable placeholder text distracts viewers from the visual design. If you fill a design mockup with actual English content, reviewers tend to focus on reading the text rather than evaluating the layout, fonts, colors, and spacing. Lorem Ipsum solves this problem because it has a natural letter distribution that mimics real text but is largely unreadable to anyone who isn't a Latin scholar – so the eye sees "text" but the brain doesn't try to read it.

Key benefits of using Lorem Ipsum:

  • Focus on visual hierarchy – headers, body text blocks, captions.
  • Test font rendering – see how a typeface looks across different weights and sizes.
  • Preview layout balance – check if columns break correctly.
  • Client presentation – avoid premature content discussions.

When Should You Use Lorem Ipsum?

There are specific scenarios where Lorem Ipsum is ideal. On the other hand, there are times when you should avoid it entirely.

✅ Appropriate times to use it:

  • Early‑stage web design mockups and wireframes.
  • Testing typography pairings and line spacing.
  • Filling blog template cards before content is written.
  • Placeholder for forms, buttons, or any UI that needs sample text.

❌ Times to avoid Lorem Ipsum:

  • Client‑facing prototypes that are close to final – it may look unpolished.
  • Accessibility demonstrations – real content is needed for screen readers.
  • SEO audits – search engines need genuine text.
  • When showing a site to a copywriter – they'll think you're finished!
