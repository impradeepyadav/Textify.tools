# Textify.tools 🚀

**Free Online Text Tools** — 70+ browser-based utilities to repeat, count, convert, encode, style, and generate text.  
No installs, no uploads, 100% private.

## 🌐 Live Website
https://textify.tools

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔁 **Text Manipulation** – Repeat, reverse, sort, split, join, truncate, and more
- 📊 **Analysis & Stats** – Word/character counter, frequency analysis, reading time estimator
- 🔐 **Encoding & Ciphers** – Base64, URL encoding, Morse code, Caesar cipher, A1Z26, JSON formatter
- 🎨 **Unicode Styling** – Bold, italic, cursive, bubble, zalgo/glitch, fancy fonts
- 🛠️ **Generators** – Passwords, UUIDs, lorem ipsum, random words/numbers/colors
- 📈 **SEO & Web Tools** – Google SERP simulator, UTM builder, slug generator
- ⚡ **Lightning Fast** – Everything runs locally in your browser
- 🔒 **Privacy First** – Your text never leaves your device; no account required
- 🌙 **Dark Mode** – Toggle between light and dark themes
- 📱 **Responsive** – Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile
