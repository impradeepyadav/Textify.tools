Why Use an Online Text Diff Checker?

Spotting every tiny change between two long texts by hand is nearly impossible. Whether you're reviewing a contract, merging code branches, or editing a manuscript, our diff checker automates the process, saving you hours of tedious manual comparison. It works entirely in your browser, so your sensitive documents never leave your device.

We designed this tool to be blazing fast and privacy‑first. Unlike desktop diff tools that require installation, or web‑based tools that upload your text to a server, Textify's diff checker runs locally using a straightforward Longest Common Subsequence (LCS) algorithm. The result is a clean, human‑readable output that you can trust.

Diff Checker vs. Traditional Comparison Methods

Many people still rely on printing two documents and manually underlining differences, or using the "Track Changes" feature in a word processor. These methods have their place, but they are slow and often limited to specific file formats. Our online diff checker gives you instant, format‑agnostic results: just copy and paste any text, and the differences are highlighted immediately. No formatting, no macros, no compatibility issues.

Best Practices for Accurate Diff Comparisons

  • Normalize whitespace first: If your texts have inconsistent spacing, run them through our Whitespace Cleaner before comparing. This prevents false differences caused by extra spaces or tabs.
  • Compare logical chunks: For very long documents, consider comparing section by section instead of the entire file at once. This makes the output easier to scan.
  • Use the download option: Save the diff as a .txt file to share with colleagues or keep for your records.
  • Pair with other tools: After spotting changes, use our Text Finder & Replacer to batch‑update recurring edits across the document.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the diff checker free?
Yes, completely free. No signup, no limits. Use it as much as you like.
How does the comparison work?
We split both texts into lines and compute the longest common subsequence (LCS). Lines present only in the original are shown in red; lines only in the revision are shown in green. Unchanged lines remain normal.
Is my text private?
Absolutely. All processing happens locally in your browser. Your text is never uploaded or stored anywhere. You can even use the tool offline once the page loads.
Can I compare large files?
Yes, but very large texts (10,000+ lines) may slow down the comparison. For most documents the diff is nearly instantaneous. If you experience slowness, try splitting the text into smaller sections.
Does it work offline?
Once the page loads, the diff engine runs entirely in your browser, so you can use it without an internet connection. Perfect for flights or remote areas.
What if the texts are completely different?
If there are no common lines, the output will show everything removed from the original and everything added in the revision — giving you a complete picture of the changes.
