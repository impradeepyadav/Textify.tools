What Are Unicode Italic Fonts?

Unicode italic fonts are special character sets that use Unicode symbols to create slanted, italic, or cursive variations of regular text. Unlike standard CSS italic formatting (which only works in rich-text environments), Unicode italic characters work everywhere вАУ text messages, social media bios, email subject lines, and even plain text documents. Each italic style uses a different Unicode block, giving you a wide variety of visual options.
