Text Truncator – Cut Text to Specific Length

Shorten any text to a precise length with a custom ellipsis. Perfect for previews, excerpts, meta descriptions, and more. All processing happens locally in your browser – 100% private.

How to Use the Text Truncator

  1. Enter your text – Paste or type the content you want to shorten into the main text area.
  2. Set the maximum length – Choose the character limit you want to apply.
  3. Select truncation mode – "Character‑based" cuts exactly at the set length. "Word‑based" cuts at the nearest word boundary to keep text readable.
  4. Customize the ellipsis (optional) – Replace the default "…" with any string (e.g., "...", "[more]", " ↻").
  5. Use extra options – Trim whitespace, or preserve the full last word (only in word‑based mode).
  6. Click "Truncate Text" – The result appears instantly in the output area.
  7. Copy or download – Use the "Copy Text" button or "Download .txt" to save the result.

What Does This Text Truncator Do?

The Text Truncator shortens your text to a specified length while intelligently handling boundaries and adding a configurable suffix. It's ideal for creating previews, meta descriptions, social media snippets, or any situation where you need text of a predictable length.

  • Character‑based truncation: Cuts the text exactly at the specified character count, then appends the ellipsis. This is perfect for strict length requirements like meta titles (60–70 characters) or meta descriptions (160 characters).
  • Word‑based truncation: Cuts at the nearest word boundary without breaking words. The result is always readable, making it great for article excerpts, email previews, or notification summaries.
  • Trim whitespace: Removes extra spaces, newlines, and tabs before truncating, giving you a cleaner result.
  • Preserve full last word: When enabled, the tool ensures that the last complete word is included before applying the ellipsis (only works in word‑based mode).

Real‑World Use Cases

  • SEO & meta tags: Create precise meta descriptions (150–160 characters) without breaking in the middle of a word.
  • Social media posts: Shorten long text to fit character limits on Twitter/X (280), LinkedIn, or Facebook previews.
  • Newsletter excerpts: Generate clean preview snippets for email campaigns.
  • Code / log summarization: Quickly compress long error messages or debug output.
  • User‑friendly previews: Show the first part of a long article or comment, with a clean "…" indicator.

Privacy & Security

All truncation happens locally in your browser. The original text is never uploaded, stored, or shared. You can cut sensitive documents, internal code, or personal notes with complete peace of mind.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Text Truncator really free?
Yes! 100% free with no limits, no registration, and no hidden fees.
What's the difference between character‑based and word‑based truncation?
Character‑based cuts exactly at the set length, which might break a word in the middle. Word‑based cuts at a space, keeping words intact for better readability.
Can I use a custom ellipsis like "..." or "[more]"?
Absolutely. Type any string in the "Custom Ellipsis" field. The default is the Unicode ellipsis "…".
Does it work on mobile?
Yes! The page is fully responsive and works on all devices including iPhone and Android.
Is my text private?
100% private. All processing happens in your browser. No data leaves your device.
