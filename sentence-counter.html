Why use this Sentence Counter?

Many academic assignments, articles, and formal documents have a minimum or maximum sentence count. This tool counts sentences using standard punctuation rules and also provides word, character, and paragraph counts. Because everything is processed locally, your content stays 100% private.

  • Sentence detection – identifies sentences ending with . ! ? and updates in real time.
  • Additional stats – also shows words, characters (with/without spaces), and paragraphs.
  • Real‑time update – counts refresh as you type or paste.
  • Privacy‑first – no data leaves your browser.

How to count sentences (step‑by‑step)

1. Paste your text

Copy the text you want to analyze and drop it into the box above.

2. Review the stats

The stat cards update instantly, showing sentence count and more.

3. Copy if needed

Click "Copy Stats" to copy a summary of all counts to your clipboard.

4. Clear and start over

Use the Clear button to wipe the text and reset all counts to zero.

Common use cases

  • Academic assignments: Ensure your essay has the required number of sentences.
  • Blogging: Monitor sentence count for readability and engagement.
  • Editing and proofreading: Quickly assess text structure.
  • Translations: Compare sentence counts between original and translated text.
  • Content requirements: Meet specific sentence limits for articles, ads, or descriptions.

Pro tips

  • The tool splits sentences at ., !, ? followed by a space or end of text. It handles abbreviations to a basic extent but may occasionally split them. If you need more control, combine with our Text Finder & Replacer to temporarily replace periods in abbreviations before counting.
  • For a complete text breakdown including reading time and unique words, use our Text Statistics tool.
  • If you only need word or character counts, our Word Counter gives you those instantly.
