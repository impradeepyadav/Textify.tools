What Is Glitch (Zalgo-Style) Text?

You may have noticed text that appears distorted or layered with unusual symbols above and below the letters. This style is commonly known as glitch or Zalgo-style text. It is created by adding special Unicode combining characters to standard letters, which results in a visually distorted effect. Textify's Glitch Mode automatically applies these characters, making it useful for creative typography, visual experiments, and decorative text styles.
