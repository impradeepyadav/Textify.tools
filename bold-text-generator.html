What Are Unicode Bold Fonts?

Unicode bold fonts are special character sets that use Unicode symbols to create bold, heavy, or black variations of regular text. Unlike standard CSS bold formatting (which only works in rich-text environments), Unicode bold characters work everywhere – text messages, social media bios, email subject lines, and even plain text documents. Each bold style uses a different Unicode block, giving you a wide variety of visual options.
