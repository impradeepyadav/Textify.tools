The Ultimate Online Text Tools Roundup

📅 March 18, 2026

In the digital age, text is everywhere – emails, social media posts, code, and content creation. Manually formatting, counting, or converting text can be tedious. That's why we've built Textify.tools – a complete suite of free, browser‑based utilities that handle all your text manipulation needs while keeping your data completely private.

In this roundup, we'll walk you through every tool currently available, how they work, and when to use them. Whether you're a developer, writer, student, or just someone who loves productivity, there's something here for you.

1. Text Repeater – Multiply Anything Instantly

Our flagship tool lets you repeat any text up to 100,000 times in a single click. Choose custom separators, apply 12 different font styles (bold, italic, cursive, bubble, wide, etc.), add decorations, and even use glitch effects. Perfect for WhatsApp pranks, stress testing, creative Instagram bios, and creating dummy data.

Try Text Repeater →

2. Word Counter – See Your Text's Anatomy

Paste or type any text and get instant counts for words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, paragraphs, average word length, reading time, and speaking time. Completely private – your text never leaves your browser.

Try Word Counter →

3. Case Converter – Fix Capitalization Fast

Convert any text to uppercase, lowercase, title case, sentence case, alternating case, or even random case. A lifesaver when you accidentally hit CAPS LOCK or need to format headlines.

Try Case Converter →

4. Text Reverser – Flip & Mirror Magic

Reverse your entire text, reverse word order, or flip characters upside down (uʍop ǝpısdn). Great for puzzles, creative messages, or just having fun with friends.

Try Text Reverser →

5. Slug Generator – SEO‑Friendly URLs in Seconds

Create clean, readable URL slugs from any headline or phrase. Automatically removes special characters, converts to lowercase, and joins words with hyphens. Essential for bloggers and content writers.

Try Slug Generator →

6. Text Sorter – Order Lines Alphabetically or Randomly

Sort lists alphabetically (A‑Z or Z‑A), numerically, or shuffle them randomly. Handy for organizing data, creating randomized lists, or preparing structured documents.

Try Text Sorter →

7. Text Diff Checker – Compare Two Texts Instantly

Paste two blocks of text side‑by‑side and instantly see every difference highlighted. Perfect for comparing code, document revisions, or spotting plagiarism.

Try Text Diff Checker →

8. Text Finder & Replacer – Bulk Find & Replace

Find specific words or phrases and replace them with another string throughout your entire text. Works with case‑sensitive matching and bulk operations. Great for editing large documents.

Try Text Finder & Replacer →

9. Character Remover – Strip Unwanted Characters

Remove specific characters, punctuation, spaces, numbers, or even emojis from your text. Clean up messy data or prepare input for other applications.

Try Character Remover →

10. Text Encoder / Decoder – Base64, URL Encoding & More

Encode or decode text using Base64, URL encoding, or binary/hex representations. Essential for developers working with web data, APIs, or debugging.

Try Text Encoder/Decoder →

11. Smart Paraphraser – Rewrite & Rephrase

Rephrase sentences while preserving their original meaning. Uses a local synonym database that works offline – your text never leaves your browser. Ideal for avoiding plagiarism or finding new ways to express ideas.

Try Smart Paraphraser →

12. Password Generator – Strong, Random Passwords

Generate cryptographically strong passwords with customizable length, including uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols. All generation happens on your device – passwords are never saved or transmitted.

Try Password Generator →

13. Lorem Ipsum Generator – Placeholder Text for Designs

Generate classic Lorem Ipsum placeholder text for mockups, layouts, or design prototypes. Choose the number of paragraphs, words, or characters. Works instantly in your browser.

Try Lorem Ipsum Generator →
