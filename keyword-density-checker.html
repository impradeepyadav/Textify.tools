What Is Keyword Density?

Keyword density is the percentage of times a particular word or phrase appears in a text compared to the total number of words. It's an important concept in search engine optimization (SEO) because it helps you understand how frequently you're using your target keywords. A balanced density signals relevance to search engines without over‑optimization.

Why Check Keyword Density?

  • Improve on‑page SEO: Ensure your main keywords appear often enough to be noticed by search engines, but not so often that you get penalized.
  • Avoid keyword stuffing: Identify overused words that might make your content look spammy.
  • Refine your writing: Spot repeated words you might not have noticed and replace them with synonyms.
  • Competitor analysis: Paste a competitor's article to see which keywords they're targeting.

Best Practices for Keyword Density

While there is no magic number, most SEO experts recommend a density of 1‑2% for your primary keyword and slightly less for secondary ones. Use variations and synonyms to keep your writing natural. Remember, quality always beats quantity — write for humans first, then use tools like this to fine‑tune.
