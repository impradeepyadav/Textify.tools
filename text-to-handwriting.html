How the Natural Jitter Effect Works

When you enable the "Natural Jitter" option, the tool adds microscopic random rotations and vertical shifts to every single character. In real handwriting, no two letters are perfectly identical — your hand naturally varies pressure, angle, and spacing. The jitter effect replicates this by applying subtle CSS transforms to each <span> element inside the preview. The result is text that looks remarkably organic, even upon close inspection. The PNG and PDF downloads capture these effects exactly as rendered.
