QR Code Generator – Turn Text into a QR Code Instantly

Enter any text, URL, or message and generate a scannable QR code right in your browser. Download it as a PNG image – completely private, no signup required.

How to Use the QR Code Generator

  1. Enter your text or URL: Type or paste the content you want to encode. It can be a website link, a message, a phone number, or any short piece of text.
  2. Adjust size and color: Choose the pixel size and pick a dark color for the QR code (the background remains white).
  3. Click "Generate QR Code": The QR code appears instantly in the area below.
  4. Download or scan: Right‑click to save the image, or use the "Download PNG" button. Then share, print, or embed the QR code wherever you need it.

Why Use This QR Code Generator?

  • 100% Private: The QR code is generated entirely in your browser. Your text or URL is never sent to a server, and no third party ever sees your data.
  • Instant & Offline: Once the page loads, you can use the tool without an internet connection. Perfect for quick, confidential QR codes.
  • Customizable: Pick any dark color and adjust the size to fit your design needs.
  • Free & No Limits: Generate as many QR codes as you like, for any purpose — personal, commercial, or educational.

Real‑World Uses for QR Codes

  • Sharing website links: Turn any URL into a QR code that people can scan with their phone camera to open the site instantly.
  • Event tickets: Embed a unique attendee ID or a ticket URL so guests can check in with a quick scan.
  • Restaurant menus: Generate a QR code that links to an online menu, a PDF, or a contactless payment page.
  • Wi‑Fi network details: Encode the SSID and password so guests can connect without typing a long passphrase.
  • Product packaging: Direct customers to a product page, instruction manual, or feedback form.

Pro Tip: For URLs, always include the full address (e.g., https://). For plain text, keep it under 2,000 characters for best scanability. The longer the text, the more complex the QR code becomes, but it still works.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the QR code generator free?
Absolutely. No signup, no hidden fees, no limitations. Generate as many QR codes as you need.
Can I generate a QR code offline?
Yes. Once the page loads, the tool works entirely in your browser. You can disconnect from the internet and still create QR codes.
What format is the downloaded image?
You can download the QR code as a PNG image, which is widely supported and easy to insert into documents, social media, or print.
Can I change the background color?
Currently, the background is always white to ensure high contrast and reliable scanning. You can change the dark color of the QR code itself.
How much text can I encode?
QR codes can store up to around 2,953 bytes of data. For plain text, that's roughly 2,000 characters. If you enter longer text, the code may become very dense but still readable by most modern phones.
Is my text private?
Yes. All processing happens on your device. Your text or URL never leaves your browser, and we do not store or track anything.
