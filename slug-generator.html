Slug Generator – Clean URL Slugs

Convert any title, heading, or phrase into a clean, SEO‑friendly URL slug. Supports single mode or bulk generation for multiple lines — all processed instantly inside your browser.

How to Use the Slug Generator

Follow these simple steps to create clean URL slugs for single titles or bulk phrases.

  • Choose mode: Select “Single Slug” for one title, or “Bulk Generate” to process multiple lines at once.
  • Enter text: Paste or type your title(s). In bulk mode, write one title per line.
  • Configure slug options: Toggle settings such as lowercase, trim spaces, remove special characters, collapse multiple hyphens, or strip stop words.
  • Generate slugs: Click the “Generate Slug(s)” button. The result appears instantly below.
  • Copy or download: Use the copy button next to each slug, click “Copy All Slugs” (bulk mode), or download the results as a .txt file.

What is a URL Slug?

A slug is the part of a URL that identifies a particular page in a readable way. For example, in https://example.com/blog/how-to-make-coffee, the slug is how-to-make-coffee. Good slugs are short, clean, and contain relevant keywords — they help search engines and users understand the page content at a glance[reference:0].

How Slug Generation Works

The tool transforms your text by:

  • Converting to lowercase (optional).
  • Removing special characters and punctuation (optional).
  • Replacing spaces and underscores with hyphens.
  • Collapsing multiple hyphens into one and trimming leading/trailing hyphens (optional).
  • Stripping common stop words such as “a”, “an”, “the”, “of”, “for”, etc., to produce shorter, more focused slugs (optional).[reference:1]

All processing happens in your browser — the text never leaves your device.

Tips for Great Slugs

  • Keep it short – aim for 3‑5 words.
  • Use hyphens to separate words (never underscores). Google recommends hyphens as word separators.
  • Avoid special characters like &, %, or punctuation.
  • Include keywords that accurately describe the page content.
  • Use lowercase to avoid accidental case‑sensitive duplication on servers.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this tool free?
Yes, completely free with no limits. No sign-up or payment required.
Can I generate slugs for hundreds of titles at once?
Absolutely. Use the Bulk Generate mode, paste each title on a new line, and the tool will instantly create a slug for every line. You can then copy all results at once or download them as a text file.
Does it work on my phone or tablet?
Yes, the page is fully responsive and works on all devices including iPhones, Android phones, and tablets.
Is my text private?
100% private. All slug generation happens locally in your browser. No data is ever sent to any server.
What are stop words and why would I strip them?
Stop words are common short words like “a”, “an”, “the”, “of”, “for”, etc. Stripping them can produce shorter, more focused slugs. Use this option when you want to create compact slugs, but be aware that sometimes those words add important context.
