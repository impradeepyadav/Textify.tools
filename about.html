About Us

Welcome to Textify.tools, a simple and reliable platform for text manipulation, repetition, and encoding tools. Our goal is to provide fast, privacy-focused, and easy-to-use online utilities that work seamlessly across devices.

Our Story

Launched in 2025, Textify.tools started with a clear purpose: to simplify repetitive digital tasks that waste time. We observed that many existing text tools were overloaded with ads, slow to respond, or required unsafe app installations. To solve this, we built a clean, web-based solution that works instantly without compromising user security.

What We Offer

Textify.tools offers a collection of practical text utilities suitable for developers, testers, and social media users:

  • Text Repeater: Instantly generate large amounts of repeated text for testing or formatting.
  • Binary & Morse Converter: Convert readable text into encoded formats and decode them back.
  • Glitch & Zalgo Text: Create visually styled text for creative and decorative use.
  • Invisible Text: Generate blank-looking characters for spacing and layout purposes.

Privacy First

Privacy is a core principle at Textify.tools. All tools run entirely client-side, meaning your text is processed directly in your browser. No input data is stored, tracked, or sent to external servers.

We hope you find our tools helpful and enjoyable to use. If you have feedback, questions, or suggestions, feel free to reach out to us anytime.
